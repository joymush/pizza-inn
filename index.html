EUNISANS PIZZA PLACE

Welcome to a family pizza place that anyone can enjoy! From family nights out to going out with your friends, Eunisans' is the perfect place to spend any night during the week or the weekend. If you want to come have a slice, then come visit us on Sweet Street, Nairobi Inspired by my love for food and people, the first Eunisans' was opened on November 19 2007.It was built upon traditional principles and commitment to quality, service, and long-standing relationships with communities, customers and employees. I grew up in an Italian neighbourhood where the emphasis on food making was its simplicity and flavor. I remember the aroma of fresh sauce being simmered at one of our neighbour's homes; the pungent smell of freshly grated cheese as Josephina sprinkled it on the pasta; and the warmth and love that exuded from the dinner table as the family ate that exuded from the dinner table as the family ate together. At Eunisans', we serve generous portions of food, made with quality ingredients by people who care. We are not about sun-dried tomatoes, wood-burning ovens, and $10 mushrooms. Eunisans' is your neighbourhood pizzeria where you can enjoy a great meal with family and friends in a clean, comfortable atmosphere at a reasonable price. If for any reason we fall to meet your expecations, please email me personally at eunisans@pizza.com. Thank you for choosing Eunisans!
